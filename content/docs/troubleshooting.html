Validation & Troubleshooting

Checklist

  • Bindings validate: Call ValidateParameterBindings() and inspect the result/logs.
  • Normalization ranges: Ensure correct min/max so 0..1 maps make sense.
  • Runtime changes: After swapping components/ASC, call RefreshParameterBindings().
  • Blueprint function binding: Does your function return float and exist on the bound object?

Common issues

  • Always zero: Wrong property/attribute name, or not replicated yet. Use handles when possible.
  • Spikes: Add light smoothing or sample at a lower frequency.
  • High cost: Lower ContextUpdateFrequency, use LOD gating for heavy features.
