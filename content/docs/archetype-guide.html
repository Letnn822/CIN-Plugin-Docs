Choose Your Archetype

Archetypes are foundational AI patterns that you extend via Blueprint inheritance. Choose the archetype that best matches your AI's primary role, then customize it for your game.

3 minutes Beginner
Philosophy: Archetypes are building blocks, not finished products. Think of them as starter templates you'll customize with your game's specific actions and behaviors.

Understanding Archetype Inheritance

How It Works: Archetypes provide a recommended starting point. When you create a UCIN_AgentPreset and select an archetype, you get pre-configured actions and services. You then extend this in Blueprint to add game-specific behaviors. 
// Archetype inheritance pattern
DA_Soldier_Preset (CIN_AgentPreset)
  └─ Archetype: BaseCombat
     ├─ Includes: Attack, TakeCover, Reload, Retreat
     ├─ Includes: PerceptionMonitor service
     └─ You add: ThrowGrenade, CallForBackup, MeleeFinisher

BP_Soldier (Blueprint, extends BaseCombat)
  └─ Customizes: Weapon type, animation sets, audio
  └─ Adds: Game-specific logic and visuals

Common Combinations

FPS Game

  • Player Enemies: BaseSquadCombat (coordinated soldiers)
  • Guards: BaseStealth (patrol and investigate)
  • Boss: BaseBoss (multi-phase encounter)

RPG Game

  • Companions: BaseCompanion (follow and assist)
  • Town NPCs: BaseNPC (dialogue and schedules)
  • Enemies: BaseCombat, BaseRanged, BaseCaster (varied combat)
  • Wildlife: BaseCreature (ambient life)

Stealth Game

  • Patrols: BaseStealth (investigation behaviors)
  • Alerted Guards: BaseCombat (when player detected)
  • Civilians: BaseNPC (avoid and react)

What You Get with Each Archetype

BaseCombat

Purpose: Foundation for combat-oriented AI with attack, defense, and tactical behaviors.

Included Actions:

  • Attack - Engage visible enemy with weapon
  • TakeCover - Move to cover when under threat
  • Reload - Reload weapon when ammo low
  • Retreat - Fall back when low health

Included Services:

  • PerceptionMonitor - Sight 3000, Hearing 2000, Damage sense

Auto-Published Inputs:

  • Health, AmmoCount, HasKnownEnemy, ThreatLevel, NearestEnemyDistance

Extend With:

  • Weapon-specific actions (ThrowGrenade, MeleeAttack, AimDownSights)
  • Cover evaluation (find best cover point)
  • Team communication (CallForBackup, ReportEnemy)

BaseSquadCombat

Purpose: Extends BaseCombat with squad coordination and tactical behaviors.

Includes Everything from BaseCombat, Plus:

  • FlankLeft/FlankRight - Coordinated flanking maneuvers
  • HoldPosition - Defensive stance
  • SuppressFire - Cover fire for teammates

Additional Services:

  • TacticalSense - Reports squad intel
  • TacticalStrategy - Leader synthesizes strategy

Additional Inputs:

  • SquadStrategy, IsSquadLeader, SquadMemberIndex, SquadEnemyCount

Extend With:

  • Formation behaviors (MaintainFormation, ReformOnLeader)
  • Advanced tactics (Bounding overwatch, room clearing)

BaseStealth

Purpose: Guards and investigators with patrol and alert behaviors.

Included Actions:

  • Patrol - Follow patrol route
  • Investigate - Check suspicious location
  • Alert - Raise alarm when enemy confirmed
  • Search - Search area for lost target

Perception Configuration:

  • Long memory: 30 seconds (remembers last known positions)
  • Enhanced hearing: 3000 units (sound-focused)
  • Sound inputs critical for investigation

Extend With:

  • Alarm systems (ActivateAlarm, CallReinforcements)
  • Environmental memory (CheckDoors, InspectObjects)
  • Communication (RadioIn, RequestBackup)
